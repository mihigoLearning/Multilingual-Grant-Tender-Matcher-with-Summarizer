TITLE: Request for Proposals: Fintech Acceleration Initiative
SECTOR: fintech
BUDGET: 5k USD
DEADLINE: 2026-03-07
REGION: West Africa

This tender supports financial technology, mobile money, digital lending, and financial inclusion. The total available funding is 5k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-03-07. In alignment with regional strategic priorities, this call for proposals invites eligible applicants to submit expressions of interest. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in West Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
