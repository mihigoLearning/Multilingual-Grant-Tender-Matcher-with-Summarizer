Call for Proposals: Agritech Innovation Fund

  • Sector: agritech
  • Budget: 5k USD
  • Deadline: 2026-03-06
  • Region: West Africa

This tender supports agricultural technology, smallholder farmer productivity, climate-smart crops, and value-chain innovation. The total available funding is 5k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-03-06. Successful applicants will enter into a performance-based grant agreement. Proposals will be evaluated on technical merit, cost-effectiveness, and alignment with stated outcomes. In alignment with regional strategic priorities, this call for proposals invites eligible applicants to submit expressions of interest. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in West Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
