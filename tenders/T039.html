Call for Proposals: Wastetech Innovation Fund

  • Sector: wastetech
  • Budget: 200k USD
  • Deadline: 2026-06-08
  • Region: Sub-Saharan Africa

This tender supports waste management, circular economy, plastic recycling, and urban sanitation. The total available funding is 200k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-06-08. In alignment with regional strategic priorities, this call for proposals invites eligible applicants to submit expressions of interest. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. All submissions are subject to due diligence and compliance review. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in Sub-Saharan Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
