West Africa Grant Programme for Cleantech Startups

  • Sector: cleantech
  • Budget: 1M USD
  • Deadline: 2026-11-03
  • Region: West Africa

This tender supports clean energy, solar off-grid solutions, energy efficiency, and renewable infrastructure. The total available funding is 1M USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-11-03. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. The contracting authority reserves the right to modify or cancel this solicitation at any stage. Successful applicants will enter into a performance-based grant agreement. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in West Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
