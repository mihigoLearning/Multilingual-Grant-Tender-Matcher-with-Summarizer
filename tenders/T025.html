Catalytic Grant — Edtech Scale-Up

  • Sector: edtech
  • Budget: 5k USD
  • Deadline: 2026-03-03
  • Region: East Africa

This tender supports educational technology, digital learning platforms, teacher training, and youth skills. The total available funding is 5k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-03-03. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. Proposals will be evaluated on technical merit, cost-effectiveness, and alignment with stated outcomes. In alignment with regional strategic priorities, this call for proposals invites eligible applicants to submit expressions of interest. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in East Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
