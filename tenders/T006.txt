TITLE: Call for Proposals: Wastetech Innovation Fund
SECTOR: wastetech
BUDGET: 200k USD
DEADLINE: 2026-03-07
REGION: West Africa

This tender supports waste management, circular economy, plastic recycling, and urban sanitation. The total available funding is 200k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-03-07. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. Proposals will be evaluated on technical merit, cost-effectiveness, and alignment with stated outcomes. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in West Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
