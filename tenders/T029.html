Open Tender: Agritech Pilot Deployment

  • Sector: agritech
  • Budget: 1M USD
  • Deadline: 2026-09-20
  • Region: Pan-African

This tender supports agricultural technology, smallholder farmer productivity, climate-smart crops, and value-chain innovation. The total available funding is 1M USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-09-20. All submissions are subject to due diligence and compliance review. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. The contracting authority reserves the right to modify or cancel this solicitation at any stage. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in Pan-African. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
