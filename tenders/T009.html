Call for Proposals: Edtech Innovation Fund

  • Sector: edtech
  • Budget: 200k USD
  • Deadline: 2026-07-16
  • Region: West Africa

This tender supports educational technology, digital learning platforms, teacher training, and youth skills. The total available funding is 200k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-07-16. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. In alignment with regional strategic priorities, this call for proposals invites eligible applicants to submit expressions of interest. Proposals will be evaluated on technical merit, cost-effectiveness, and alignment with stated outcomes. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in West Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
