TITLE: Call for Proposals: Healthtech Innovation Fund
SECTOR: healthtech
BUDGET: 1M USD
DEADLINE: 2026-02-24
REGION: East Africa

This tender supports digital health, telemedicine, maternal and child health, and medical supply chains. The total available funding is 1M USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-02-24. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. Successful applicants will enter into a performance-based grant agreement. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in East Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
