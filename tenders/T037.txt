TITLE: Open Tender: Edtech Pilot Deployment
SECTOR: edtech
BUDGET: 200k USD
DEADLINE: 2026-11-10
REGION: East Africa

This tender supports educational technology, digital learning platforms, teacher training, and youth skills. The total available funding is 200k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-11-10. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. In alignment with regional strategic priorities, this call for proposals invites eligible applicants to submit expressions of interest. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in East Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
