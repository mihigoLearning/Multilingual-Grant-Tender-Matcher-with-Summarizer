TITLE: West Africa Grant Programme for Cleantech Startups
SECTOR: cleantech
BUDGET: 200k USD
DEADLINE: 2026-07-10
REGION: West Africa

This tender supports clean energy, solar off-grid solutions, energy efficiency, and renewable infrastructure. The total available funding is 200k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-07-10. Proposals will be evaluated on technical merit, cost-effectiveness, and alignment with stated outcomes. All submissions are subject to due diligence and compliance review. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in West Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
