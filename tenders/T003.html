Request for Proposals: Healthtech Acceleration Initiative

  • Sector: healthtech
  • Budget: 1M USD
  • Deadline: 2026-09-17
  • Region: West Africa

This tender supports digital health, telemedicine, maternal and child health, and medical supply chains. The total available funding is 1M USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-09-17. Proposals will be evaluated on technical merit, cost-effectiveness, and alignment with stated outcomes. In alignment with regional strategic priorities, this call for proposals invites eligible applicants to submit expressions of interest. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in West Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
