TITLE: Call for Proposals: Edtech Innovation Fund
SECTOR: edtech
BUDGET: 1M USD
DEADLINE: 2026-03-20
REGION: West Africa

This tender supports educational technology, digital learning platforms, teacher training, and youth skills. The total available funding is 1M USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-03-20. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. All submissions are subject to due diligence and compliance review. Proposals will be evaluated on technical merit, cost-effectiveness, and alignment with stated outcomes. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in West Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
