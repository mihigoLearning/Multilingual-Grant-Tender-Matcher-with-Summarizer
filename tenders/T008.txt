TITLE: East Africa Grant Programme for Healthtech Startups
SECTOR: healthtech
BUDGET: 200k USD
DEADLINE: 2026-12-08
REGION: East Africa

This tender supports digital health, telemedicine, maternal and child health, and medical supply chains. The total available funding is 200k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-12-08. The contracting authority reserves the right to modify or cancel this solicitation at any stage. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in East Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
