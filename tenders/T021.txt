TITLE: Request for Proposals: Healthtech Acceleration Initiative
SECTOR: healthtech
BUDGET: 5k USD
DEADLINE: 2026-02-10
REGION: Pan-African

This tender supports digital health, telemedicine, maternal and child health, and medical supply chains. The total available funding is 5k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-02-10. The contracting authority reserves the right to modify or cancel this solicitation at any stage. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. Successful applicants will enter into a performance-based grant agreement. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in Pan-African. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
