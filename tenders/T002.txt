TITLE: East Africa Grant Programme for Edtech Startups
SECTOR: edtech
BUDGET: 5k USD
DEADLINE: 2026-03-19
REGION: East Africa

This tender supports educational technology, digital learning platforms, teacher training, and youth skills. The total available funding is 5k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-03-19. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. The contracting authority reserves the right to modify or cancel this solicitation at any stage. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in East Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
