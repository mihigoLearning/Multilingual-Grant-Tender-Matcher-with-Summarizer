TITLE: Catalytic Grant — Cleantech Scale-Up
SECTOR: cleantech
BUDGET: 5k USD
DEADLINE: 2026-11-26
REGION: East Africa

This tender supports clean energy, solar off-grid solutions, energy efficiency, and renewable infrastructure. The total available funding is 5k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-11-26. The contracting authority reserves the right to modify or cancel this solicitation at any stage. Successful applicants will enter into a performance-based grant agreement. Applicants must demonstrate organisational capacity, financial probity, and a proven track record. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in East Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
