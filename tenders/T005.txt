TITLE: Request for Proposals: Cleantech Acceleration Initiative
SECTOR: cleantech
BUDGET: 200k USD
DEADLINE: 2026-09-23
REGION: East Africa

This tender supports clean energy, solar off-grid solutions, energy efficiency, and renewable infrastructure. The total available funding is 200k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-09-23. In alignment with regional strategic priorities, this call for proposals invites eligible applicants to submit expressions of interest. All submissions are subject to due diligence and compliance review. Successful applicants will enter into a performance-based grant agreement. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in East Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
