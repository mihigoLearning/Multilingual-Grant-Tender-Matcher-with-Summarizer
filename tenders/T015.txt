TITLE: Call for Proposals: Agritech Innovation Fund
SECTOR: agritech
BUDGET: 200k USD
DEADLINE: 2026-10-17
REGION: West Africa

This tender supports agricultural technology, smallholder farmer productivity, climate-smart crops, and value-chain innovation. The total available funding is 200k USD. Proposals must be submitted before 2026-10-17. Successful applicants will enter into a performance-based grant agreement. Shortlisted candidates may be invited to a clarification interview. Proposals will be evaluated on technical merit, cost-effectiveness, and alignment with stated outcomes. Eligible applicants: registered enterprises with 2+ years of operation, 5-50 employees, operating in West Africa. Priority for women-led and youth-led ventures.
